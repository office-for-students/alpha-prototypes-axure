How to choose a course

The survey is an independent, UK-wide survey that gathers final year students opinions about their academic experience of higher education universities and colleges.


Students answer questions on teaching, learning, the organisation of courses and how well they are resourced as well as what support is available to them.


We use the results of this survey on our course information pages.



If you want to talk to current students or others making their decisions, there are a number of online forums and social media options available.

You can talk to current and future students about a whole range of things on the forums at The Student Room.






Universities and colleges often have their own social media channels for connecting applicants and current students, so look out for those as well.
