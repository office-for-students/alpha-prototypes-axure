How to choose a course

Tuition fees don’t just cover the cost of teaching you.

They’ll also include other things that the university or college needs to provide to enable you to complete your course.


These include libraries, online learning facilities, student support services and course administration.


Other course costs

These might include costs for field trips or materials. Some of these can be fixed, but sometimes they can vary significantly, for example, the costs of materials for a fine art course.


Universities and colleges should include on their tuition fee for the course and any additional costs that you will need to pay on their course pages.


They should also give an indication of the cost of materials you’ll need to buy yourself, where this is relevant.


You can follow the Course costs link on our course pages to quickly find the cost of the course.
